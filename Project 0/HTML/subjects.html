  1. Astronomy :

    Astronomy is the only field of study at Hogwarts that has a direct equivalent in the Muggle world.

  2. Charms

    Charms is the type of spells concerned with giving an object new and unexpected properties, and hence this class mainly consists on learning those sorts of spells.

  3. Defence against Dark Arts

    Defence Against the Dark Arts, commonly shortened to D.A.D.A., is the class that teaches students defensive techniques to defend against the Dark Arts, and to be protected from Dark creatures.

  4. Flying

    Flying is the class that teaches the use of broomsticks made for the use of flying and is taught only to Hogwarts first years.

  5. Herbology

    Herbology is the study of magical plants and how to take care of, utilise and combat them. There are at least three greenhouses, holding a variety of magical plants of varying degrees of lethality.

  6. History of Magic

    History of Magic is the study of magical history. Cuthbert Binns' lessons are depicted as some of the most boring at Hogwarts. They are only lectures, given without pause, about significant events in wizarding history.

  7. Potions

    Potions is described as the art of creating mixtures with magical effects. It requires the correct mixing and stirring of ingredients at the right times and temperatures.

  8. Transfiguration

    Transfiguration is the art of changing the form or appearance of an object, and hence this is what this class teaches.

  9. Arithmancy

    Arithmancy is a branch of magic concerned with the magical properties of numbers.

  10. Divination

    Divination is the art of predicting the future. Various methods are described, including tea leaves, Fire-omens, Crystal balls, Palmistry, Cartomancy , astrology, and Dream interpretations.

  11. Muggle Studies

    Muggle Studies is a class taught by Charity Burbage which involves the study of the Muggle (non-magical) culture "from a wizarding point of view." It also includes Muggle Art and Muggle Music.

  12. Study of Ancient Runes

    The Study of Ancient Runes is a general theoretical subject that studies ancient runic scripts.
